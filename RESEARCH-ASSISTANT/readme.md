# 🔬 ResearchPilot AI – Intelligent Research Assistant

ResearchPilot AI is an AI-powered research assistant designed to help students, researchers, and professionals begin and organize their research effectively. Built using **Python**, **Streamlit**, and **Google Gemini API**, the application acts as a virtual research mentor by providing structured guidance, research methodologies, recommended tools, and actionable roadmaps for any research topic.

Unlike traditional chatbots, ResearchPilot AI focuses on teaching users **how to conduct research**, encouraging critical thinking and systematic problem-solving.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 📚 AI-powered research guidance
* 📝 Step-by-step research roadmaps
* 🎯 Topic-specific research recommendations
* 🔍 Research methodology suggestions
* 🛠️ Tool and resource recommendations
* 💡 Motivation with practical reality checks
* 💬 Interactive conversational interface
* 📖 Session-based conversation history

---

## ✨ How It Works

1. Enter a research topic or question.
2. The AI analyzes your query.
3. Generates a structured research plan.
4. Recommends appropriate research methodologies.
5. Suggests useful tools, datasets, and academic resources.
6. Maintains conversation history throughout the session.
